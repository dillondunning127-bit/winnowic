How does the SAT Math diagnostic work? +

The free diagnostic is a 20-question test covering all major SAT Math units: Algebra, Advanced Math, Problem Solving & Data Analysis, and Geometry & Trigonometry. After completing it, you get a predicted SAT Math score range and a unit-by-unit accuracy breakdown showing exactly where you are losing points. It takes about 12–15 minutes and requires no account.

Winnowic has 500+ SAT Math practice questions across all four SAT Math domains. Free users have access to a limited practice bank. Winnowic Premium ($6/month) unlocks the full question bank including hard difficulty questions across every unit.

What SAT Math topics does Winnowic cover? +

Winnowic covers all four SAT Math domains:

Algebra — linear equations, systems of equations, inequalities, linear functions

Problem Solving & Data Analysis — ratios, percentages, statistics, scatter plots, data interpretation
Geometry & Trigonometry — angles, triangles, circles, right triangles, trigonometric ratios

How accurate is the SAT score prediction? +

Winnowic provides a predicted SAT Math score range (e.g. 580–620) based on your accuracy across all units.
